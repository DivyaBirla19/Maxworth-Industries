About Us
We at Maxworth Industries are manufacturing various types of Ferro Alloys and Steel Castings as well. Our production facility which is located on the East coast of India near West Bengal is equipped with two furnaces which can help us manufacture the same. We also have stores in Andhra Pradesh to help us reach out to all parts of India.
We have been supplying Ferro Alloys to some of the biggest and most well known steel plants in India, like TATA Steel, Jindal Steels , Steel Authority of India Limited and many more. At present we are supplying to the export market through several traders in UAE.
